About OVKB

Thoughtful digital art and designs for meaningful moments.

Oshadha Balasuriya is the creative mind behind Designs by OVKB, an emerging digital art initiative focused on thoughtful and heartfelt design. His journey began with an interest in digital creativity, which gradually developed into a dedicated pursuit of creating meaningful greeting cards and visual artwork.

Through patience, care, and close attention to detail, Oshadha creates designs that convey warmth, sincerity, and emotional connection. Each piece reflects his commitment to authenticity and his desire to create something truly meaningful.

As he continues to grow and refine his work, Oshadha hopes to share Designs by OVKB with a wider audience, offering creations that bring joy, comfort, and connection. His beloved Beagle, Broody, remains a constant and inspiring companion by his side.
