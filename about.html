Behind the Art

A journey of sincerity, creativity, and the joy of digital storytelling.

The Creative Mind

Oshadha Balasuriya is the creative heart behind Designs by OVKB, an emerging digital art initiative focused on thoughtful and heartfelt design. His journey began with a simple curiosity for digital creativity, which blossomed into a dedicated pursuit of creating meaningful greeting cards and visual artwork.

Through patience, care, and a keen eye for detail, Oshadha crafts designs that convey warmth, sincerity, and emotional connection. Each piece reflects his commitment to authenticity and his desire to create something truly meaningful.

As he continues to grow and refine his work, Oshadha hopes to share Designs by OVKB with a wider audience, offering creations that bring joy, comfort, and connection. His beloved Beagle, Broody, remains a constant and inspiring companion by his side.
